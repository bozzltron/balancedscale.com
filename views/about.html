Balanced Scale Media LLC was founded my Michael Bosworth in 2008 to offer proffesional grade web applications at price range that small businesses could afford. The company serves as a contractor for web development as well as constultant for all things technical.

The name "Balanced Scale" comes from a time when scales were used to ensure the integrity in an exchange of goods. In the same, way Balanced Scale Media is about offering proffesional services with integrity.
